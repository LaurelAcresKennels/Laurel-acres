About Laurel Acres Kennels!

  • A Premier Dog and Cat Boarding Kennel

    Welcome to Laurel Acres Kennels, where Hillsboro and surrounding area dogs and cats love to get away from it all.

    We offer the finest accommodations in a state of the art facility, where you know your pet is getting pampered while you are away; our goal is to provide quality care in a safe environment!

    Open since 1986 and renovated in 2005, Laurel Acres Kennels has a long history of caring for your pets. As a member of The Pet Care Services Association, we subscribed to their code of ethics. Many of our employees are “Certified Pet Care Technicians," and have also completed the Pet First Aid Training.

    We know that your pet is a valued member of your family. All of us here at Laurel Acres Kennels are committed to doing everything we can to make your pet's stay as safe, comfortable, and enjoyable as possible until your return.

    Please give us a call to make a reservation or if you have any questions.

  • Our state of the art facility:

    Kitty Condos:

    We offer "Kitty Condos" for your feline companions. Our separate heated and air conditioned cattery ensures that your kitty will have a comfortable stay.

    Dog Runs:

    Dog runs are ventilated and climate controlled. They are fully air conditioned for the extremely hot summer days. For the colder months there is in-floor radiant heat, meaning the heat is located where the dogs are resting, sleeping and playing.

    Visit our gallery page where you can see what a typical visit consists of from your pets’ point of view. Our goal is to make their stay as positive as possible!
