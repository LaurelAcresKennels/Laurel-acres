Making Reservations:

Make your reservations early, as we book quickly during the holidays and summer months. Let us know the date you intend to drop off your pet and the date you intend to pick them up. Please provide phone numbers and contact names where someone can be reached in case of emergency, or if we simply have a question.

Be sure to let us know of any needs or special problems your pets may have such as; hearing loss, fear of thunder or fireworks, excessive barking, allergies or aggression problems, etc. This ensures we are able to provide the best care possible.

Food:
We feed premium Diamond Brand Maintenance dry dog food. We also have Kirkland wet food, Diamond Brand dry cat food, and Friskies wet food. If your pet is on a special diet, please feel free to bring your own food and any special feeding instructions.

Bedding & Toys:
We do provide bedding for your pet, but if your pet has special bedding, please feel free to bring it. All we ask is that it can be laundered if needed.
Bring special toys or anything else that will make your pet feel at home. We have found, through experience, that certain toys can be hazardous to pets and we reserve the right to restrict any toys or treats that may do so.

Medications:
We know there are times when a pet may need medication, and we are happy to administer it for you. Please bring medication, dosing directions, and any special instructions your vet may have provided.
