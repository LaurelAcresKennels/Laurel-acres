Services & Rates at Laurel Acres Kennels:

 
We pride ourselves in our ability to care for any pet in an individual and specialized way.

Please feel free to call or email us at any time to discuss any special needs your pet may have.

 

We are happy to work with you and decide the best way to care for your pet while they are at our facility.

 

 

 

Daily Boarding

Dogs have indoor/ outdoor accommodations. Indoor runs are heated and air conditioned and bedding is provided. Outdoor runs are covered. Dogs have access to both areas during the day and are housed indoors at night.
Cats are housed in our separate, quiet Cattery.

 
Dogs: $28.00
Cats: $17.00

Doggie Daycare

This plan provides calendar day boarding during our business hours and includes a Play Yard activity for more social dogs.  A different activity may be given to those dogs that prefer to be alone. Any additional dogs will be charged at $23.00 per day.

 

Dogs, Full Day: $29.00
Dogs, Half Day [4-hrs or less]: $16.00

Cat Daycare

Cats may also come for calendar day visits. Daycare includes one activity of your choosing.

 

Cats, Full Day: $17.00

 

Nature Walk

Your dog is walked on a leash by a Certified Pet Care Technician through a nature path and our sculpted lawn. (15 minutes)

 

Dogs: $9.00

Brush & Hug

Your pet is held, spoken to quietly and brushed gently by a Certified Pet Care Technician. Highly recommended for the pet who loves being fussed over.

(15 minutes)

 
Dogs: $9.00
Cats: $9.00

Snack & Snuggle

Homemade doggie ice cream will be given to your pet while they are held and spoken quietly to by a Certified Pet Care Technician.

(15 minutes)


Dogs: $9.00
Cats: $9.00

Mouse Hunt

Your kitty is played with and tempted with mouse toys by a Certified Pet Care Technician.
(15 minutes)

 

Cats: $9.00

Play Yard

Takes place in one of 3 large fenced areas where your dog will receive extra exercise and make new friends. Single dogs or families who don’t enjoy playing with other dogs may enjoy a separate Play Yard.
(15 minutes.)

 

Dogs: $9.00

Romp & Rassle

Ball and Frisbee playtime with a Certified Pet Care Technician. Highly recommended for the athletic, active dog.
(15 minutes)

 

Dogs: $9.00

Splish & Splash

Only available in the summer months, as weather permits. Includes playtime in water with a Certified Pet Care Technician. Highly recommended for dogs who love the water. (15 minutes)

 

Dogs: $9.00

Potty Walks

Especially recommended for senior dogs and puppies. Aids in housebreaking training for puppies. Also great for older dogs to encourage them to be active without overexerting themselves. (As long as necessary.)

 

Dogs: $5.00

VIP Package

"Very Important Pooch".
DOGS: Daily Walk, Daily Play Yard, and your choice of a one of the following (Daily Brush & Hug or Romp & Rassle or Snack & Snuggle). CATS: Daily Brush & Hug, Daily Snack & Snuggle, Daily Mouse Munt.


Dogs: $23.00
Cats: $23.00

Senior Package

Three potty walks daily and either a Daily Brush & Hug or Snack & Snuggle. Benefit: Keeps older dogs active and reduces stress with structured activity.

 

Dogs: $20.00

Puppy Package

Two Potty Walks Daily, Daily Play Yard, and one more daily activity of your choice. Benefit: Handling throughout the day is great for socialization!

 

Dogs: $24.00

Medication

If your pet needs medication administered, our Certified Pet Care Technicians will give them what they need. (Price per dose.)

 
Dogs: $1.00
Cats: $1.00

Injections

If your dog requires an injection such as insulin, our Certified Pet Care Technicians will give them what they need. (Price per injection.)

 

Dogs: $5.00
Cats: $5.00

Pickup & Delivery

By appointment, our staff can pickup and deliver your pet to your home or airport. Call and ask for price quote. (Per hour with a $25.00 minimum).

 

Dogs: $50.00
Cats: $50.00

Bathing

Baths start at $25.00. Prices go up depending on the breed. Ask for a quote! A bath includes trimming nails and cleaning ears. Call to schedule. We do not bathe cats.

 

Dogs: $25.00 and up.
Ask for a quote.

Nail Trims

One of our certified Pet Care Technicians can safely trim your dog’s nails during their stay. We also offer cat nail trims. Call to schedule.

 

Dogs: $10
